The point of the at-scale view: a solid left edge makes the flagged rows scan as a column of marks — the eye catches the set (and the gaps) in peripheral vision, even with many flagged. A is the cleanest column (implemented); B adds a body cue but risks the "everything's tinted" noise; C is softer but the gaps between pills make the column slightly less continuous.
